About Us

We are a group of people focused on the field of basic, applied and clinical research, innovating in the world of nanobiotechnology and artificial intelligence, who faithfully believe in the link between medicine and the digital world, looking for a strong interaction between science and people surfing the web 3.0.

We are a group of researchers eager to help people suffering from specific diseases, not only by providing or investigating new alternative treatments but also by generating patient networks to generate support groups. On the other hand, our investors generate a point of profitability while entering the world of science, technology and innovation.

MISSION

Our fundamental mission is to put science and technology at the service of society, through the production of a relevant scientific activity, active transfer of knowledge and production of innovation in the field of modern biotechnology that contributes to the well-being of society.

VISION

At Biobayter we aspire to be one of the most advanced companies in the area of science and technology; carry out the most innovative ideas to contribute to the production of new molecules that will help the diagnosis and treatment of different diseases, supported through high quality standards and the participation of the best human talent.

In 5 years we see ourselves with an exponential growth in the development of our research lines and innovating in other areas of biomedicine, biotechnology and the digital world.

VALUES

RECTITUDE (ETHICS, RESPECT FOR PEOPLE AND VOCATION TO SERVICE)

At BioBayter we care about the needs of society, acting within the framework of ethical principles in clinical and human research. Biobayter's actions are aimed at the needs of society and we understand that our work is a service to the development of science.

QUALITY (IMPROVEMENT, TRAINING AND EXCELLENCE)

BioBayter is characterized by facing the social, ethical and economic challenges that arise in the field of science, mainly in less favored countries. We have a multidisciplinary team that is constantly training to fulfill BioBayter's mission.

INNOVATION (COLLABORATION, TEAMWORK AND ASSERTIVENESS)

At BioBayter we actively dialogue with all the agents involved in our areas of interest and collaborate with those who make decisions, to achieve improvements in the environment of the biotechnology sector by providing knowledge, commitment, loyalty and promoting innovative ideas that have a positive impact on society.

Participates in the first NFT project that has an altruistic character and a long-term future.
