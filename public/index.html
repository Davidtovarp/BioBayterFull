About Us
Who We Are

Biobayter is a company focused on the execution of innovative scientific projects that contribute to the diagnosis, improvement of existing treatments and cure of different diseases.

Providing quality research focused on improving the life style of patients.

Linking basic and applied research and clinical research with fields such as Biotecnology and Artificial intelligence. We are in the stage of seeking financing through national and international calls and donations that allow us to carry out the bank of projects that we currently have, related to Cancer, Biotechnology, Mesenchymal Stem Cells, Bioinformatics and Epidemiology.
