About Us

We are a group of researchers focused on the field of basic, applied and clinical research, innovating in the world of nanobiotechnology and artificial intelligence, who faithfully believe in the link between medicine and the digital world.

We are a group of researchers:

  • Eager to help people suffering from specific diseases
  • Providing or investigating new alternative treatments
  • Generate patient networks and generate support groups

    • With the help of our investors, our NFT BioBayter will provide a source of income while contributing to the world of science, technology and innovation.

    MISSION

    Our fundamental mission is to put science and technology at the service of society, through the production of a relevant scientific research, active transfer of knowledge and production of innovation in the field of modern biotechnology that contributes to the well-being of society.

    VISION

    At Biobayter we aspire to:

    1. Be one of the most advanced companies in the area of science and technology.
    2. Carry out the most innovative ideas to contribute to the production of new treatments.
    3. Help the diagnosis and treatment of different diseases through high quality standards.

    In 5 years we see ourselves with an exponential growth in the development of our research lines and innovation in other areas of biomedicine, biotechnology and the digital world.

    VALUES

    RECTITUDE (ETHICS, RESPECT FOR PEOPLE AND VOCATION TO SERVICE)

    At BioBayter we care about the needs of society, acting within the framework of ethical principles in clinical and human research. Biobayter's actions are aimed at the needs of society and we understand that our work is a service to the development of science.

    QUALITY (IMPROVEMENT, TRAINING AND EXCELLENCE)

    BioBayter is characterized by facing the social, ethical and economic challenges that arise in the field of science, mainly in less favored countries. We have a multidisciplinary team that is constantly training to fulfill BioBayter's mission.

    INNOVATION (COLLABORATION, TEAMWORK AND ASSERTIVENESS)

    At BioBayter we actively collaborate with professionals involved in our areas of interest to achieve improvements in the biotechnology sector by bringing knowledge, commitment, loyalty, and innovation in benefit of society.

    Participates in the first NFT project that has an altruistic character and a long-term future.
